Biography:

Who's Drake?

Aubrey Drake Graham was born on October 24, 1986 in Toronto (Canada), where he was raised, and is a celebrated rapper,
songwriter, actor and businessman. Currently Drake is one of the biggest names in contemporary hip hop.

How It All Started

In the world of music, the rapper released his first mixtape called Room for improvement in 2006.
The following year it was time to promote Comeback Season and began to receive support from illustrious producers such as Kanye West, Lil Wayne and Jay-z.
At that time Drake was already leaning more towards the world of music than acting.
As an actor, he made few appearances in one-off series such as The Border (2008) and Being Erica (2009).

The Rapper's Career Climbing

Em 2009 Drake assinou contrato com a Young Money de Lil Wayne tendo lançado "So far gone".
Dessa época são os sucessos "Best I ever had" e "I'm going' In".
No ano a seguir foi a vez de promover o seu disco "Thank me later".
Empreendedor, Drake fundou o Very Own (OVO), um coletivo sediado em Toronto que virou gravadora em outubro de 2012.
